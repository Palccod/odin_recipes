Stuffed French Toast

Stuffed French Toast Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cream cheese
  • Jam
  • Eggs and milk
  • Almond extract
  • Butter

How to Make Homemade Stuffed French Toast

  1. Spread cream cheese on four slices of bread and spread jam on the other four slices.
  2. Form sandwiches with the bread, then dip the sandwiches in a mixture of eggs and milk.
  3. Cook the sandwiches in butter until golden brown on both sides.
